Joel L. Callao

TITLE: Hibok Panlantaw (titig na tahimik)

DESCRIPTION: A painting blend with classical and modern stylistic portraiture. This is not only a realistic art painting, but it is also a portrait painting with deep meaning. This woman has a joyful eyesight, her eyes and lips smile a little bit filled with self-confidence, chaos, resilience and humble demeanor. This painting symbolizes someone who is deep in thought and not easy to read their emotions. Her outfit blends modern and old-style clothing. Her hat with a pink flower is an elegant vintage design, along with a modern denim vest and plaid sleeves. This combination shows an appreciation for tradition while embracing a modern identity. It is a representation of a woman who is not afraid to create uniqueness in her outfit and create her own style instead of following trends. The reason the artist chose a blurry background is because he wants to emphasize the image and also to get the viewer’s attention focusing on this woman.

Sculpure

Xyla Beatrice P. Amores

DESCRIPTION: For my final project, I used clay to sculpt my art because it is easy to mold. The design I chose to carve was a duck sitting peacefully in a green scenery. The subject represents me who likes to be alone and at peace, focusing on reflecting about myself and the decisions I made in my life.Through this artwork— I can help everyone that having some time to yourself will help you recharge again. Overall, I wanted to make this artwork truly for myself, that sometimes it is okay to be with you only, not with others—just by yourself. This artwork can be a constant reminder that being alone helps us to reconnect from ourselves, reflect, and our purpose in life.

Josephine P. Teorica

DESCRIPTION: This free-standing sculpture is made of colorful clay shaped into flowers rising from a base of soft, rounded blocks. The bottom part represents the different experiences that have supported me in life. Each sphere has its own shade and texture, just like the mixed emotions and lessons I have gone through. The bright flowers on top symbolize my dreams and the parts of myself that continue to grow. Their upright form shows my strength and determination. The colors reflect my hope, positivity, and creativity. Even with small imperfections, the sculpture stands firm just like me when I face challenges. It reminds me that growth does not need to be perfect to be meaningful. This artwork shows my journey of becoming stronger, more confident, and more open to new possibilities. Through this piece, I see myself blooming little by little.
